Chicken & Dumplings

Serves: 4

Prep Time

20 min

Cook Time

5 hr 30 min

Total Time

5 hr 50 min

Ingredients

  • 4-6 boneless Chicken Thighs
  • 2 tablespoon Butter
  • 2 cans Cream of Chicken Soup
  • 1 can Chicken Broth
  • ½ teaspoon Poultry Seasoning
  • ¼ teaspoon Onion Powder
  • ¼ teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • ⅛ teaspoon White Pepper
  • ½ teaspoon Season All
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 tablespoon dried Parsley
  • 4 Grands Flaky Refrigerator Biscuits

Utensils

  • Slow Cooker

Preparation

  1. Wash and dice onion. Place chicken into slow cooker and then add butter, cream of chicken soup, chicken broth, seasonings, onions, and parsley.
  2. Cook on high for 4-6 hours TIP: Add more broth for longer times.
  3. Using a fork, break up chicken into pieces.
  4. Cut biscuits into 9 pieces and stir into hot chicken.
  5. Cook for another 30 minutes on high. Divide between bowls.
