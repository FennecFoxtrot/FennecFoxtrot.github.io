Oven-Baked Potatoes

Serves: 4

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

40 min

Total Time

55 min

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of little Potatoes
  • 2 teaspoon dried Garlic
  • 2 teaspoon Parsley
  • Season All
  • Onion Powder
  • Cooking Oil

Utensils

  • Baking Pan
  • Aluminum Foil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F°. Wash and dry all produce. Half potatoes.
  2. Add a large drizzle of cooking oil to baking pan. Add potatoes and toss. Add Season All and onion powder to taste, and toss potatoes again. Add garlic and parsely.
  3. Cover baking pan with aluminum foil and bake in oven for 30 minutes, or until potatoes are soft. Once softened, bake uncovered for another 10 minutes. Serve.
