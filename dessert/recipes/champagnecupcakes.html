Champagne Cupcakes

Serves: 24

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

15 min

Total Time

25 min + 10 min

Ingredients

  • 1 15.25 ounce box Betty Crocker Supreme Moist Cake Mix
  • 1 cup Pink Champagne
  • ⅓ cup softened Butter
  • 3 Eggs
  • 16 ounce Pillsbury Creamy Supreme White Frosting
  • ½ cup Gold & Silver Sprinkles

Utensils

  • Mixer
  • Muffin pan
  • Cupcake liners
  • Piping bag

Preparation

  1. Start by mixing the cake mix, butter, eggs, and champagne. Mix with a mixer on high until well-blended.
  2. Pour mixture into muffin pan with liners, and bake according to box instructions.
  3. Let cool completely. Fill piping bag with icing and place 1M tip on the end. Start in the middle and work your way out and up with icing. Apply sprikles and serve.
