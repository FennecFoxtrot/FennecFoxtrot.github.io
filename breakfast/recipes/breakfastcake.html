Breakfast Cake

Serves: 12

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

30 min

Total Time

45 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Brown Sugar
  • ¾ cup Sugar
  • 2½ cup Flour
  • ½ cup plain Applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon Nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 2 teaspoon Cinnamon
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Baking Soda
  • 1 cup Buttermilk (Make Your Own)

Utensils

  • Large bowl
  • Small bowl
  • 9x13 Baking pan

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F°. In a large bowl, combine sugars, flour, oil, nutmeg, and salt. (Mixture will be slightly lumpy.)
  2. Move ¾ cup of the mixture into small bowl and add cinnamon. Set aside to use as crumb topping. Add egg to the original mixture, then add baking soda, baking powder, and buttermilk.
  3. Pour batter into greased baking pan. Sprinkle crumb topping over batter, and then bake 25-30 minutes. Allow cake to cool completely before cutting. (TIP: Freezes well for leftovers.
