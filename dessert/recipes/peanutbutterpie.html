Creamy Peanut Butter Pie

Serves: 8

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

-

Total Time

15 min + 4 hr

Ingredients

  • 2 packages (8 ounces each) softened Cream Cheese
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • ⅔ cup Creamy Peanut Butter
  • ⅔ cup Whipped Topping
  • 14 Peanut Butter Cups
  • 1 9-inch Chocolate Crumb Crust

Utensils

  • Small bowl

Preparation

  1. In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, sugar, and peanut butter until light and fluffy. Fold in whipped topping. Coarsley chop half of the peanut butter cups and; stir into cream cheese mixture.
  2. Spoon into crust. QUarter remaining peanut butter cups; arrange over top. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
