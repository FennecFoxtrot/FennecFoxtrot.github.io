Mozzarella Zucchini Rounds

Serves: 2

Prep Time

5 min

Cook Time

20 min

Total Time

25 min

Ingredients

  • 1 Zucchini
  • ¼ cup Panko Breadcrumbs
  • ½ cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tuscan Heat Spice
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Utensils

  • Baking Sheet

Preparation

  1. Adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 450 F°. Wash and dry all produce. Trim and slice zucchini into ⅓-inch-thick rounds.
  2. Toss zucchini on a baking sheet with a large drizzle of olive oil, Tuscan Heat Spice, salt, and pepper. Roast on top rack until golden brown and tender, 14-16 minutes.
  3. Once zucchini is tender, remove sheet from oven. Heat broiler to high. Sprinkle zucchini with panko and mozzarella. Broil until browned and crispy, 2-5 minutes. TIP: Watch carefully to avoid burning.
