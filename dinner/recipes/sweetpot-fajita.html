Sweet Potato Fajitas

with Pickled Jalapeno and Lime Sour Cream

Serves: 2

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

25 min

Total Time

35 min

Ingredients

  • 2 Sweet Potatoes
  • 1 Jalape単o
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Roma Tomato
  • 1 Lime
  • 2 tablespoon Sour Cream
  • 6 Flour Tortillas
  • 1 cup Shredded Cheese of Choice (Cheddar and Pepper Jack are good
  • ½ teaspoon Sugar
  • Southwest Spice Blend
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Utensils

  • Baking Sheet
  • Small Bowl
  • Paper Towel
  • Zester
  • Large Pan

Preparation

  1. Adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 425 F°. Wash and dry all produce. Cut sweet potatoes into ½-inch-thick wedges. Toss on a baking sheet with a large drizzle of oil and Southwest Spice. Season with salt and pepper. Roast on top rack until tender and lightly crisped, 25-30 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, thinly slice jalape単o into rounds, removing ribs and seeds for less heat. Zest and quarter lime. Halve, peel, and thinly slice onion. Dice a few slices until you have ¼ cup. Halve, core, and thinly slice green pepper into strips. Dice tomato.
  3. In a small bowl, combine jalape単o, juice from half the lime, ½ tsp sugar, and a big pinch of salt. Set aside to pickle, stirring occasionally, until ready to serve.
  4. Heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add sliced onion and green pepper. Cook, stirring occasionally, until softened and lightly charred, 8-10 minutes. (TIP: Add a splash of water if veggies begin to brown too quickly.) Season with salt and pepper. Remove pan from heat.
  5. While veggies cook, in a second small bowl, combine tomato, diced onion, and a squeeze of lime juice. Season with salt and pepper. In a third small bowl, combine sour cream, lime zest, and a squeeze of lime juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Divide tortillas between plates and fill with sweet potatoes, veggies, cheese, salsa, lime sour cream, and as much pickled jalape単o (draining first) as you like. Serve with any remaining lime wedges on the side.
