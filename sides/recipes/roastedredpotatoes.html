Roasted Red Potatoes with Bacon & Cheese

Serves: 4

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

55 min

Total Time

1 hr 10 min

Ingredients

    • 2 pounds small Red Potatoes
    • ½ cup Ranch Dressing
    • ½ cup shredded Cheddar Cheese
    • ¼ cup Bacon Bits
    • Parsley
    • Cooking Oil

Utensils

  • Baking Pan
  • Large Bowl
  • Aluminum Foil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F°. Wash and dry all produce. Quarter potatoes.
  2. Mix dressing, cheese, and bacon bits in a large bowl. Add potatoes and toss lightly.
  3. Lightly grease baking pan with cooking oil. Spoon potatoes and dressing mixture into pan and cover with foil.
  4. Bake 40 minutes covered. Uncover and bake an additional 15 minutes or until tender.
  5. Sprinkle with parsley and serve.
