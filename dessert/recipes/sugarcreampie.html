Hoosier Sugar Cream Pie

Serves: 10

Prep Time

15 min

Cook Time

1 hr

Total Time

1 hr 15 min

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Whipping Cream
  • 1 cup Half-and-Half Cream
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • ½ cup Flour
  • 1 unbaked 9-inch Pie Shell
  • 3 tablespoon Unsalted Butter
  • 2 teaspoon Vanilla
  • Cinnamon
  • Nutmeg

Utensils

  • Large bowl
  • Aluminum foil

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 425 F°. Cut butter into small dots.
  2. In bowl, whisk together cream, half-and-half, vanilla, sugar, flour, and a pinch of cinnamon. Pour into pie shell. Dot top evenly with butter; sprinkle with nutmeg and cinnamon. Bake in oven 15 minutes.
  3. Remove pie from oven. Reduce heat to 350 F°. Wrap edges of crust with aluminum foil to prevent burning, return pie to oven and bake 45 minutes more.
  4. Let cool on rack; pie will set upon standing. Serve at room temperature.
