Lasagna

Serves: 8

Prep Time

30 min

Cook Time

35 min

Total Time

1 hr 5 min

Ingredients

  • 1 pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 teaspoon Minced Garlic
  • 16 ounce can cut-up Tomatoes
  • 8 ounce can Tomato Sauce
  • 6 ounce can Tomato Paste
  • 2 teaspoon dried Basil
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • 8 ounce Lasagna Noodles
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2½ cup Ricotta Cheese
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 tablespoon dried Parsley
  • 1 pound grated Mozzarella Cheese

Utensils

  • Large Pan
  • Large Pot
  • Small Bowl
  • Baking Pan

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 F°. Bring a large pot of salted water and 1 TBSP copking oil to a boil. Wash and dry onion. Dice onion.
  2. Heat a large drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef, diced onion, and garlic and cook until beef is browned. Drain off fat.
  3. Stir in undrained cut-up tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and basil. Cover and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Meanwhile, cook noodles until tender in boiling salted water, 9-11 minutes. Drain and rinse noodles.
  5. Beat eggs in a small bowl; add ricotta cheese, ½ cup Parmesan cheese, parsley, and 1 tsp salt. Mix together until creamy. Layer half of the noodles in a baking pan. Spread with half the ricotta filling, mozarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers and sprinkle remaining Parmesan on top.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or till heated through. Let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
