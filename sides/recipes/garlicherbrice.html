Garlic Herb Rice

Serves: 2

Prep Time

0 min

Cook Time

20 min

Total Time

20 min

Ingredients

  • ¾ cup Rice
  • 1 tablespoon Garlic Herb Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Utensils

  • Rice Cooker (or Small Pot)

Preparation

  1. Add rice and 1 cup water to rice cooker and cook. If a rice cooker isn't available in a small pot, combine rice, 1 cup water, and a big pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a low simmer. Cook until rice is tender, 15-18 minutes.
  2. Fluff rice with a fork; stir in garlic herb butter. Taste and season with salt and pepper. Serve.
