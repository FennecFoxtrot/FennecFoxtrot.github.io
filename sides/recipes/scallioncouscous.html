Scallion Couscous

Serves: 2

Prep Time

5 min

Cook Time

10 min

Total Time

15 min

Ingredients

  • 2 Scallions
  • ½ cup Couscous
  • Cooking Oil
  • Chicken Stock
  • Salt

Utensils

  • Small Pot

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry all produce. Trim and thinly slice scallions, separating whites from greens.
  2. Heat a drizzle of oil in a small pot over medium-high heat. Add scallion whites; cook until softened, 1 minute. Add couscous; stir until toasted, 30 seconds. Add ¾ cup water, a splash of stock, and a pinch of salt. Cover and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to low. Cook until couscous is tender, 6-8 minutes. Serve.
