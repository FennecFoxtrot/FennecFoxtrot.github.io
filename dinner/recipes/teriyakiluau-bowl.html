Teriyaki Pork Luau Bowl

with Kiwi Salsa & Lime Crema

Serves: 2

Prep Time

10 min

Cook Time

25 min

Total Time

35 min

Ingredients

  • 1 Kiwi
  • 1 Bell Pepper
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Roma Tomatao
  • 1 teaspoon minced Garlic
  • 1 cup Rice
  • 1 Lime
  • 2 tablespoon Sour Cream
  • 10 ounce Ground Pork
  • 8 tablespoon Teriyaki Sauce
  • 2 tablespoon Butter
  • 1 teaspoon Sugar
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Utensils

  • Rice Cooker or Small Pot
  • Peeler
  • Small Bowl
  • Zester
  • Medium Bowl
  • Large Pan

Preparation

  1. Wash and dry all produce. Peel and dice kiwi. Dice tomato. Halve, peel, and thinly slice onion; finely dice a few slices until you have 2 TBSP. Zest and quarter lime. Halve, core, and thinly slice bell pepper crosswise into strips.
  2. Melt 1 TBSP butter in rice cooker. Add rice and stir to coat. Stir in 1½ cup water and a big pinch of salt, then cook. If a rice cooker isn't available melt 1 TBSP butter in a small pot over medium-high heat. Add rice and stir to coat. Stir in 1½ cup water and a big pinch of salt. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce to a low simmer. Cook until rice is tender, 15-18 minutes. Keep covered off heat until ready to serve.
  3. While rice cooks, in a medium bowl, combine kiwi, tomato, diced onion, and a squeeze of lime juice. Season with salt and pepper. In a small bowl, combine sour cream, half the lime zest, and a pinch of garlic to taste. Stir in water 1 tsp at a time until mixture reaches a drizzling consistency. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Heat a drizzle of oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add green pepper and sliced onion; season with salt and pepper. Cook, stirring, until tender and lightly browned, 6-7 minutes. Add remaining garlic and stir until fragrant, 30 seconds. Transfer veggies to a plate.
  5. Heat a drizzle of oil in pan used for veggies over medium-high heat. Add pork; season with salt and pepper. Using a spatula, press into an even layer. Cook, without stirring, until browned on bottom, 3-4 minutes. Break up meat into pieces and continue cooking until pork is cooked through, 1-2 minutes. Return cooked veggies to pan. Stir in teriyaki sauce, 1 tsp sugar, and 1 TBSP butter. Cook until thickened, 1-2 minutes more.
  6. Fluff rice with a fork; stir in remaining lime zest and season with salt. Divide between bowls and top with pork mixture, kiwi salsa, and lime crema. Serve with remaining lime wedges on the side.
