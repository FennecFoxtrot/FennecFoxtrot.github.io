Ginger Scented Carrots

Serves: 2

Prep Time

5 min

Cook Time

25 min

Total Time

30 min

Ingredients

  • 12 ounce Carrots
  • ½ thumb Ginger
  • Cooking Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Utensils

  • Peeler
  • Baking Sheet

Preparation

  1. Adjust rack to top position and preheat oven to 425 F°. Wash and dry all produce. Trim, peel, and cut carrots on a diagonal into ½-inch-thick pieces. Peel and mince or grate ginger.
  2. Toss carrots on a baking sheet with a drizzle of olive oil, ginger, salt, and pepper. Roast on top rack until tender and lightly browned at the edges, 20-25 minutes. Serve.
