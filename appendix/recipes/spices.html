Spices

Berbere Spice Blend

  • 3 parts paprika
  • 1 part cayenne (I don't add this)
  • ¼ part ground ginger
  • ⅛ part ground cardamom
  • ⅛ part ground fenugreek

Cajun Spice Blend

  • 2 parts paprika
  • 2 parts onion powder
  • 1 part garlic powder
  • 1 part dried oregano
  • 1 part dried thyme
  • ½ part dried basil
  • ½ part cayenne (I don't add this)

Fall Spice Blend

  • 3 parts dried thyme
  • 3 parts ground sage
  • 2 parts garlic powder
  • 1 part onion powder

Fry Seasoning

  • 1 part garlic powder
  • 1 part onion powder
  • 1 part paprika

Meatloaf Seasoning

  • 2 parts onion powder
  • 2 parts garlic powder

Shawarma Spice Blend

  • 2 parts tumeric
  • 2 parts cumin
  • 1 part garlic powder
  • 1 part paprika
  • ½ part ground allspice
  • ½ part black pepper

Smokey BBQ Seasoning

  • 8 parts smoked paprika
  • 6 parts granulated sugar
  • 2 parts garlic powder
  • 1 part dry mustard
  • 1 part ground cumin
  • 1 part ground ginger
  • ½ part black pepper

Southwest Spice Blend

  • 4 parts garlic powder
  • 2 parts cumin
  • 2 parts chili powder

Tunisian Spice Blend

  • 4 parts ground caraway seed
  • 4 parts paprika
  • 4 parts tumeric
  • 4 parts chili powder
  • 4 parts garlic powder
  • 2 parts ground cinnamon
  • 1 part cayenne (I don't add this)
  • 1 part black pepper

Turkish Spice Blend

  • 2 parts cumin
  • 2 parts garlic powder
  • ¼ part ground allspice
  • ¼ part chili flakes

Tuscan Heat Spice

  • 4 parts dried basil
  • 2 parts dried rosemary
  • 2 parts dried oregano
  • 2 parts garlic powder
  • 1 part cayenne (I don't add this)
  • 1 part ground fennel
